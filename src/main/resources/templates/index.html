Welcome to NCL

National Cybersecurity Research & Development Lab

 

 

NCL is a shared national infrastructure that provides computing resources, repeatable and controllable experimentation environments, as well as application services for the cybersecurity R&D community. The infrastructure includes a cluster of 300 nodes that provides a wide range of provisioning mechanisms, hosting of security data and security services. NCL aims to provide a platform that fosters and encourages collaboration among researchers in academia, the industry as well as government bodies both locally and internationally through the sharing and validation of research outcomes.

 

The laboratory is funded by the National Research Foundation (NRF) since November 2015.

Join Us

 

NCL invites applications for a few research positions in the area of cyber security, including Research Fellows, Research Assistants and Student Interns (undergraduate and graduate).

Applicants should possess knowledge or experiences in one or more cybersecurity research areas (e.g. system/software/web/mobile security), networking, virtualization and/or big data systems, etc.

You will be working with the team together to make the testbed and its associated various services up and fully ready for use. You will have chance to work on cloud provisioning technologies (e.g. OpenStack), big-data management systems (e.g. HDFS), and various advanced and latest topics in cyber security.

For more information, please contact: Assoc. Prof Chang Ee-Chien changec@comp.nus.edu.sg
