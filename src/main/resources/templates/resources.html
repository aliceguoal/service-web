External Links

 

Learn more about cybersecurity research and development in Singapore.

  • IMPACT

    The Information Marketplace for Policy and Analysis of Cyber-risk & Trust (IMPACT) program, funded by US Department of Homeland Securities, supports the global cyber risk research community by coordinating and developing real world data and information sharing capabilities. IMPACT Datasets are available for use by all researchers based in academic universities in Singapore.

    Visit the website to create an account and browse the datasets available. Contact support@ncl.sg for any help you require.

  • SingCERT

    SingCERT responds to cyber security incident for its Singapore constituent. It was set up to facilitate the detection, resolution and prevention of cyber security related incidents on the Internet.

  • iTrust

    iTrust researchers focus on the development of advanced tools and methodologies to ensure security and safety of current and future cyber physical systems and Internet of Things (IoT) systems.

  • TSUNAMi

    TSUNAMi center examines how trustworthy software can be built from commercial off-the-shelf (COTS) software components via ingenious combinations of analysis, testing, verification, hardening, isolation and system design.
