Step 1

Fill in a valid email address, your preferred password, and accept the Acceptable Usage Policy and Terms & Conditions, then click on Next.

Step 2

Fill in all required information here. ALL fields are required.

Step 3

Choose to create a new team, or to join an existing team.

Step 4

To create a new team, fill in the required information such as Team Name, Team Description and Team Website.

Select your organization type from the drop-down menu.

Select if you want your team to be public or private.

Step 5

To join an existing team, simply enter the team name to join. A list of public teams can be viewed at the footer of the website.
